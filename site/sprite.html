While in Mexico in 2016, I started a sprite animation project to help fund the repair of the sails for my boat pino.

The sprites are in the style of oquonie, and all are requests by people on Twitter(back when I had an account) and Mastodon.

I still take sprite requests, but I no longer do animations. I make both digital and physical commissions. Hand-drawn commissions are drawn in ink on 300 gsm textured paper. Prices are 40$ for a single B&W character(plus extra if requesting complex added details, like a pet for example), and 60$ for a physical inked drawing(plus shipping, varies depending on where you live).
