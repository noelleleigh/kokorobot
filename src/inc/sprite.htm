While in Mexico in 2016, I started a sprite animation project to help fund the repair of the sails for my boat {Pino}.

The sprites are in the style of {Oquonie}, and all are requests by people on Twitter(back when I had an account) and Mastodon.

I still take sprite requests, but I no longer do animations. I make both digital and physical commissions. Hand-drawn commissions are drawn in ink on 300 gsm textured paper. Prices are 50$CAD for a single B&W character(plus extra if requesting complex added details, like a pet for example), and 70$ for a physical inked drawing(plus shipping, varies depending on where you live).
